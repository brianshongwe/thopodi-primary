Why Choose US?

From the Preparatory Principal In the Preparatory School, we have dedicated teachers who recognise that education goes beyond the classroom. Within our warm family environment, we attempt to instill a set of core values enabling our pupils to participate in the school and the broader community as responsible, caring and productive human beings. In the words of Sir Arthur Helps, ‘Nothing succeeds like success’. All pupils are carefully guided towards experiencing successful outcomes leading to a healthy and safe learning environment.
